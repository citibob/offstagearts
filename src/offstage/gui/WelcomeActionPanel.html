OffstageArts Modules

The functionality of OffstageArts is organized into easy-to-use modules that correspond to areas of activity within an arts organization: Development, School, Accounting and Front Desk. You can always always switch between modules using the "Window" menu.

You may also go directly to the modules using the links below

  • Development: General contact management, mailing lists, reports. Use this module if you need to view or edit your list of contacts, track donations, or send a marketing communication.
  • School Setup: OffstageArts functionality for setting up open and closed enrollment terms. May also be used for managing rehearsals or any other recurring meeting.
  • School: Registration of students in closed enrollment terms.
  • Front Desk: Typically, the work-study at the front desk will sit in front of this screen. It is used to sign in students for closed and open enrollment, but does not allow general browsing or changes to the database.
  • Accounting: This screen allows your accountant to fix accounting mistakes and synchronize data with external accounting packages such as QuickBooks.
